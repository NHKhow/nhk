2019-06-17

绘制世界城市散点图

library(maps)
library(ggplot2)
data(world.cities);
head(world.cities)

绘制中国城市散点图

China<- subset(world.cities, country.etc == "China")
ggplot(China, aes(long, lat)) +
geom_point(colour = alpha("red", 1))
